# Borsht - Instant Pot 

## Total Time - 1 hour

* **Prep Time**: 30 minutes
* **Cook Time**: 30 minutes (plus pressure release)

## Servings

* 6–8 servings

---

## Ingredients

**Vegetables:**

* 2 medium **beets**, peeled and grated or julienned
* 2 medium **carrots**, peeled and grated
* 1 medium **yellow potato**, peeled and diced
* 1 medium **yellow onion**, finely chopped
* 1/2 small **cabbage**, shredded
* 2–3 cloves **garlic**, minced

**Broth & Flavor:**

* 6 cups **vegetable or beef broth**
* 2 tablespoons **tomato paste**
* 1 tablespoon **olive oil**
* 2 teaspoons **salt** (or to taste)
* 1/2 teaspoon **black pepper**
* 1 tablespoon **white vinegar** or lemon juice (for brightness)
* 1 **bay leaf**
* Optional: 1 teaspoon **sugar** (balances acidity)

**Toppings:**

* **Sour cream**
* Fresh **dill**, chopped

---

## Instructions

### 1. **Sauté the Aromatics**

* Turn Instant Pot to **Sauté mode**.
* Add **olive oil**, then sauté **onions** until soft (3–4 minutes).
* Add **garlic** and sauté 1 more minute.
* Stir in **tomato paste** and cook for 1–2 minutes to deepen the flavor.

### 2. **Add Main Ingredients**

* Add **grated beets**, **carrots**, **potatoes**, **cabbage**, and **bay leaf**.
* Pour in the **broth**.
* Add **salt**, **pepper**, **vinegar**, and optional **sugar**.
* Stir well.

### 3. **Pressure Cook**

* Close the lid, set valve to **Sealing**.
* Cook on **Manual / High Pressure** for **10 minutes**.
* Allow **10 minutes natural release**, then carefully **quick release** the remaining pressure.

### 4. **Finish & Serve**

* Open the lid, stir, and **taste** for seasoning — adjust salt, vinegar, or sugar if needed.
* Ladle into bowls and top with a **dollop of sour cream** and a sprinkle of **fresh dill**.

---

## Variations

* **Meaty Version**: Add **1/2 lb of stew beef**, cubed, before pressure cooking. Sauté it first for added flavor.
* **Vegan**: Skip sour cream or use a plant-based alternative.
* **Spicier**: Add a pinch of cayenne or some chili flakes.
