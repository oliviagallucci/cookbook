# Brown Rice - Instant Pot 

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup rice (rinsed until the water runs mostly clear)
* 1.25 cups water

**Steps**

1. Rinse your rice — yes, really. It keeps it fluffy, not gluey.
2. Dump rice + water into Instant Pot.
3. Close lid, set valve to *Sealing*.
4. Press **Pressure Cook -> High** → **22minutes**.
5. Let it **natural release for 10 minutes**, then vent.

Fluff with fork.
