# Pumpkin Puree by Nomante 

(See [YT Short](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0Pv7gj77z0&source_ve_path=MTc4NDI0))

## Ingredients 
- 1 small pumpkin

## Steps 
- Stab pumpkin with a fork 6+ times
- Microwave pumpkin 3-5 minutes 
- Cut pumpkin in half
- Put parchment or baking paper on a baking sheet
- Place pumpkins on the sheet face down
- Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes (skin should easily peel off and fork should go right in)
- [Puree it](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0Pv7gj77z0&source_ve_path=MTc4NDI0)
